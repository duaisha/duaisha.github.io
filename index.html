About

I am a Machine Learning Researcher at Mercedes-Benz R&D India, specializing in Computer Vision, Synthetic Data, Domain Adaptation, and Human Pose Estimation in automotive settings. My work focuses on building robust ML solutions—efficient, scalable, and production-ready—to bridge the simulated and real-world domains.
